Killua Zoldyck

About Killua

Killua is the third child of Silva and Kikyo Zoldyck and the heir of the Zoldyck Family, until he runs away from home and becomes a Rookie Hunter. He is a trained assasin. He is the best friend of Gon Freecss, and is currently traveling with Alluka Zoldyck.

Killua's Characteristics

  1. He has spiky silver hair, pale skin and blue eyes
  2. He is cheeky and mischievous
  3. He is rude to strangers and older people
  4. He loves sweets
  5. He is quick thinking
  6. He can be violent, deadly and bloodthirsty (all you want in a man ;) )
